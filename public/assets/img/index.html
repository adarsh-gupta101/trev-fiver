When we opened our doors in 2017, we were just two dedicated individuals that wanted to transform the custom software,IT-infrastructure and website development experience. In the process of making big changes in the technology scene, we saw a transformation of our own. Our growth plan was simple - be good people. That's it.

We've grown to 10+ employees and completed over 200 projects for more than 150 clients across Zimbambwe and the Sadc region. We've built strong relationships with startup entrepreneurs and established companies by developing quality solutions with transparency. Most importantly, after five years of success, we still believe that being good people is the best marketing strategy.
